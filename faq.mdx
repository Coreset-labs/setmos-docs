## Is Setmos available to everyone?

No. Access is subject to jurisdiction, investor status, sanctions screening, wallet policy, onboarding review, and product-level restrictions.

## Is the demo dashboard an investment offer?

No. The demo is a product preview and does not constitute an offer to sell or a solicitation to buy any security, token, fund interest, or investment product.

## What products are expected at launch?

The current launch set includes setTSY, setMM, setCRD, setLIQ, setALT, and setG.

## What is the settlement rail?

The dashboard is designed around USDC settlement.

## What network does Setmos use?

Setmos uses the Stellar blockchain as core infrastructure.

## Are issuers visible?

Investor docs may identify issuers and providers where helpful for trust, diligence, and onboarding. The app may present a simplified Setmos-branded product view.

## Can approved access be revoked?

Yes. Access may be limited, suspended, or revoked due to jurisdiction, sanctions, wallet risk, product restrictions, document requirements, or other compliance concerns.

## Where are final product terms defined?

Final terms are defined in product-level legal, issuer, subscription, offering, custody, and onboarding documents.
