Setmos provides eligible investors with a single dashboard for accessing tokenized allocation options across launch categories including short-term treasuries, money market exposure, private credit, institutional liquidity, alternative income strategies, and investment-grade gold.

The current app is a demo environment. Access is limited to reviewed users and is subject to jurisdiction, eligibility, onboarding, sanctions screening, wallet policy, and product-level restrictions.

## How Setmos works

Setmos uses one onboarding path across launch options. Investors move through jurisdiction review, KYC/AML, wallet and eligibility checks, dashboard access, and the subscription and redemption engine.

<img src="/images/investor-flow-setmos.png" alt="Setmos investor access flow and asset custody structure" />

## Eligibility and jurisdictions

Setmos availability is not universal. Products may be unavailable to users in prohibited jurisdictions, users subject to sanctions or high-risk activity flags, users acting for restricted persons, and users who do not meet product-specific eligibility requirements.

<Card title="Important" icon="shield-alert">
  The demo dashboard is not an offer to sell or a solicitation to buy any security, token, fund interest, or investment product. Access, investment, transfer, and redemption are subject to final onboarding, legal documents, issuer requirements, and applicable law.
</Card>

## Custody and token model

Native product tokens are maintained under issuer-side arrangements. Set tokens on Stellar are supported through third-party custody and operational providers. Transfers may be subject to wallet allowlists, transfer controls, sanctions screening, and product-specific restrictions.
